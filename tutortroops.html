Introducing WASCAA's new initiative to provide affordable tutoring services: "Where All Students Can Achieve Anything"

    Below are our current tutors available to help K-12 students succeed with their school coursework, SAT/ACT testing, writing skills, general advice about school and college application process, and more.

    Lakshin Kumar

    Hello! My name is Lakshin Kumar and I am the CEO of WASCAA. I am a senior in high school and have experience with many subjects. I love working with kids of all ages and have worked as a private tutor and teacher in many subjects, mainly in areas of STEM subjects. Teaching has always been a passion of mine, and seeing a student understand something that they did not before always brightens both their day and mine. Thank you so much for your interest in our program!
    Fast Facts:
    • ACT: 36 composite
    • SAT Subject tests: 800 Math 2 & 790 Biology
    • Commended National Merit Scholar
    • Subjects interested: Math, Biology, English, ACT/SAT, SAT II

    Allan Dao

    Hi! My name is Allan Dao and I am a senior in high school that has been active in WASCAA since the organization first started out. I enjoy teaching and learning about a variety of subjects, but as a STEM-oriented student I am most comfortable with teaching math and computer science and would love to tutor and work with students of all ages. My previous work within teaching includes being an instructor for computer science based summer camps and elementary after-school classes, and my favorite part of these experiences is whenever I am able to help a student understand a new concept — it's always a joy to see a student smile after accomplishing a difficult task.
    Fast Facts:
    • ACT: 35 Composite, 11 writing
    • SAT Subject tests: 800 Math 2
    • Commended National Merit Scholar
    • Subjects interested: Math, ACT/SAT, SAT Math II, Basic Computer Science

    Amogh Pande

    Hi! My name is Amogh Pande, and I am a senior at Inglemoor High School. While I may be young, I am a high-achieving and driven student-athlete with a lot of experience pertaining to teaching and volunteering. My joy for teaching stems from my mom- who is a high school teacher at North Creek High School. I enjoy tutoring other students of all age groups, and have worked with organizations such as the YMCA and City of Kenmore to help younger children in my community. From help on essay-writing to playing the piano, I would love to help! I believe that every student has a different learning style and different needs- and it's up to me as a tutor to find and define these styles and needs, so I can be the best tutor possible.
    Fast Facts:
    • ACT: 36 Composite (36R, 35M, 36S, 36E, with 12/12 on essay)
    • SAT: 1590 (800 Reading/Writing, 790 Math)
    • SAT Subject Tests: 800 Math II, 800 Literature, 790 Biology (Ecology)
    • Commended National Merit Scholar
    • Subjects Interested: ACT/SAT, English, History, Biology, Public Speaking, Piano
