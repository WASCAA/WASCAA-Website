It all starts here.

Health Horizons at UW Seattle

Health Horizons was an event that WASCAA at the University of Washington put on for fellow UW students. WASCAA was fortunate to have a fantastic panel of healthcare leaders ready to share their experiences and lead a Q&A about a variety of topics and pathways in the medical field.

WASCAA+

WASCAA+ was WASCAA's former initiative to provide quality tutoring services for an affordable price. Our tutors were all talented students from the University of Washington. Profits directly contributed to our goals as a nonprofit.
