Jarek Cruz

Operations Committee - Secretary, Photographer, Human Resources Lead

Personal Biography

Jarek is currently a senior at Inglemoor High School. He is heavily involved within his community and is a part of many clubs, including FBLA and HOSA. He has played the viola for over half a decade, and is apart of the Inglemoor Orchestra. He is a two sport athlete, participating in both track and football. He has played football for 7 years, and has been a varsity starter on the high school team for 2. In his free time, Jarek enjoys taking photos and watching videos on Youtube. Through WASCAA, Jarek hopes to create a positive impact in his community by helping others get more involved in spreading awareness about a prevailing issue. He recognizes the impact cancer can have on the families involved, and is motivated to contribute to the cause. In the future, Jarek hopes to pursue a career in business or in journalism.
