Personal Biography

Anna Li is currently a senior at Inglemoor High School. She was born in Seattle, Washington and has lived in Kenmore her whole life. She currently is active in school activities such as DECA, HOSA, and the Symphonic Band. From these she has developed professional, collaborative, and teamwork skills that she can apply to her extracurriculars. Ever since she was four, Anna has danced at the Hengda Dance Academy. Even to this day her passion for dance perseveres as a major part of her life because of the lessons of dedication, hard work, and resilience that it has taught her. On another note, Anna has always had a strong interest in science and the medical field: “I have always loved how science can uncover discoveries about other parts of life and allow one to understand the inner workings of things.” In 2016, one of her close family relatives was diagnosed with Stage 3 colon cancer. Thus,Anna has personally witnessed the severity of cancer and how it negatively affects a individual and the people around them. “I hope that during my time in WASCAA I can work my hardest to help spread awareness among the youth of America about how we can help and protect the people around us from diseases like cancer.”
