Marketing Committee - Social Media Lead

Personal Biography

Divya Akkiraju is currently a junior attending Inglemoor High School. As well as contributing to a good cause, WASCAA, provides an incredible learning process for her personally. Being the youngest in the group, she has had the opportunity to learn from and connect with some of the brightest minds in her local area to bring change to the world. She has learned about putting together websites and filing taxes, the importance of social media and communications, as well as working as part of a team. Within her school, she participates in DECA and Speech and Debate and is on track to graduate with an IB diploma in 2021. Her goal for the future is to go into the law department of a major tech company, combining some of my biggest passions: computer science, business, and law. Outside of the classroom, she is a competitive athlete, having participated in national-level volleyball and premier soccer.
