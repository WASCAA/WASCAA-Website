Our Mission

Mission Statement

Washington Student Cancer Awareness Association, or WASCAA for short, is a solely student run organization based in Washington State around the Puget Sound Area. Collectively, the WASCAA team includes a variety of students of different backgrounds and skills, but we are united by a common desire: to promote student awareness about the cancer community and to help fundraise to support the community. One of our end goals is to serve as a platform to foster student collaboration towards unique ways that students in Washington can directly impact the cancer community.
